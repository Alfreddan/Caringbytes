About CaringBytes

CaringBytes is a student-run coding club at Caringbah High School dedicated to using technology for social good. Founded in 2017 by ex-student Gabriel Toh, It is an excellent opportunity for those who share a passion for coding to get together and discuss personal projects, work on a current club project or even learn to code from scratch!

Our mission is to empower students with technical skills while fostering a sense of social responsibility. We believe technology should be used to solve real-world problems and make our world a better place.

Our Mission

To create a community of student developers who use their skills to address social challenges through innovative technology solutions.

Our Vision

A world where technology is accessible to all and used as a force for positive change in society.
